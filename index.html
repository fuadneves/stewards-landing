Common questions

Things parents ask us first.

Is this recognised?

Yes — IGCSEs are accepted by universities and sixth forms internationally. It's the same qualification a British international school student would sit.

What about timezones?

Live sessions run in scheduled bands that work across multiple regions. Where attendance isn't possible live, recordings are available for asynchronous catch-up.

How involved do parents need to be?

Meaningfully. Not as teachers — as partners. We'll walk you through exactly what that looks like in the admissions conversation.

What does it cost?

Fees vary by region, and need-based support is available. Talk to admissions and we'll be honest with you about the numbers.

Can my child join mid-year?

Sometimes. It depends on the year group and current capacity. Ask us — we'll tell you straight.
