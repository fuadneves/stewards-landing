Common questions

Things parents ask us first.

Is this recognised?

Yes — IGCSEs are accepted by universities and sixth forms globally. It's the same qualification a British international school student would sit.

What about timezones?

Live sessions run in two main bands — one Europe/Africa-friendly and one Americas-friendly. Recordings are available for asynchronous catch-up.

How involved do parents need to be?

Meaningfully. Not as teachers — as partners. We'll walk you through exactly what that looks like in the onboarding call.

What does it cost?

Fees vary by region, and need-based support is available. Book a call and we'll talk numbers honestly.

Can my child join mid-year?

Sometimes. It depends on the year group and current capacity. Ask us — we'll tell you straight.
